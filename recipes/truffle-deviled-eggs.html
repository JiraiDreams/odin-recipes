Truffle Deviled Eggs

Description

A luxurious twist on a classic appetizer, these combine a smooth blend of mashed yolks, mayonnaise, dijon mustard, and truffle oil.

Ingredients

  • 6 large hard-boiled eggs
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise, or as needed
  • 1 teaspoon Dijon mustard
  • 1 teaspoon truffle oil
  • 1/8 teaspoon truffle salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon minced chives
  • 12 pieces shaved truffles (optional)

Steps

  1. Halve eggs lengthwise and scoop egg yolks into the bowl of a mini food processor; set egg whites aside. If you do not have a mini food processor, you can mash all ingredients in a bowl with a fork.
  2. Add mayonnaise; Dijon mustard, truffle oil, salt, and pepper to the egg yolks; blend well until smooth, scraping down the sides of the bowl. If mixture is too thick, blend in an extra teaspoon or two of mayonnaise.
  3. Place yolk mixture into a resealable bag and snip off a corner. Pipe mixture into egg whites. Top with chives and shaved truffle, and refrigerate until ready to serve.
